⚠ Before You Join — Hardware Check
Minimum specs for streaming + voice
🖥
CPU
Intel Core i5 4th Gen / AMD Ryzen 3 1000 series or newer
Below this: emulator may stutter under OBS load. Expect dropped frames and audio desync.
💾
RAM
8GB minimum — 16GB recommended
Below 8GB: OBS + emulator + browser may compete for memory. Crashes likely.
📶
Upload Speed
5 Mbps minimum — 10 Mbps recommended
Below 5 Mbps: stream buffers for viewers. Reduce OBS bitrate to 1500 Kbps at 480p.

OBS Settings (retro)
Bitrate: 2500 Kbps · 1280×720 · 30fps · x264
